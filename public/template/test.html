Description

Price Drop!!! Beautifully upgraded 2-story Craftsman style bungalow and corner lot minutes from City Park, Golf Course, Denver Zoo, Nature & Science Museum, Downtown Denver, Light Rail, Bus Lines, I-70 and more! This well cared for home boasts 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms and a 2-car oversized garage with private driveway and additional off-street spaces. Original hardwoods and Victorian woodwork accents throughout, gas fireplace and an enclosed porch/sun room! Over 55K kitchen remodel down to the studs with gleaming quartz countertops, custom cabinetry, beetle kill pine wood flooring and an eat-in breakfast nook with storage! Finished basement with custom ceiling lighting, exposed brick wall, wide plank hardwood flooring and a bedroom with full bathroom - perfect for a rental! Owned solar panels by Namaste Solar come with huge energy and utility bill savings! Enjoy the second story balcony overlooking the private fenced backyard. Urban farmer's paradise with fruit trees, gooseberry bush, grapes, raspberries, black berries, raised beds for vegetables, perennials and more. Don't miss this turnkey move-in ready home with endless possibilities!
